Africa<br><br>

Africa idunhu guru rinobatanidza nyika dzakawanda. Ruzhinji rwenyika idzi rwunogara vanhu vechitema vanotaura<br> 
mitauro yakasiyana-siyana yechizvarirwo chavo. Vachena vanowanikwawo mune dzimwe dzenyika idzi  kuburikidza <br>
nekupambwa kwaive kwakaitwa Africa nenyika dzekuEurope.  

NechekuMaodzanyemba ndiko kunowanikwa vanhu vechiArab vane ganda rakatoredzera revachena. Nyika dziri ikoko <br>
dzinosanganisira Egypt, Algeria, Morocco Chad neTunisia. KuChamhembe kuzere vanhu vechitema avo vanowanikwa <br>
munyika dzinosanganisira Mozambique, Malawi, Zambia, Swaziland, South Africa, Lesotho, Botswana neZimbabwe.<br>
kuburikidza nekupambwa kwaive kwakaitwa Africa nenyika dzekuEurope. Pakati peAfrica nekuMavirira kwayo kune <br>
dzimwewo nyika dzakawandisa zvikuru zvekuti hatikwanise kudzidoma dzese panguva ino.

Nyika dzine huwandu hwevanhu hwakanyanya ndedzinoti Nigeria, Ethiopia, Egypt, DRC neSouth Africa. Zvizvarwa<br>
zveNigeria, Ethiopia neDRC zvakatekesehera nenyika dzakawanda dzemuAfrica nepasi rose. Izvi zvinonyanyokonzerwa<br>
nekutsvaga mikana yemabasa anopa pundutso negoho guru.
